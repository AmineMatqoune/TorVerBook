User Stories:

1-As a user, I want to contact* a book-seller, so that I can buy a book.
	contact*: via e-mail, phone number, chat.

2-As a user, I want to highlight the ads, so that I can have more chance to sell the book.

3-As a user, I want to contact via e-mail, phone number and chat the book-seller, so that I can meet him
and buy the book.
